Why Aegis ID fits

Every sensitive action can create evidence.

Aegis ID can sit beside a Power Pages or portal experience as the assurance layer: who signed in, what credential they presented, what challenge they approved, and when the event happened.

Identity proof

Request Microsoft Verified ID or another wallet credential before disclosing restricted services.

Action proof

Send an Aegis wallet challenge for consent, submission, approval, or acknowledgement.

Hardware proof

Require YubiKey or platform passkey step-up for higher-risk administrative or signing actions.
