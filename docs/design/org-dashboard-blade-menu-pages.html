Dashboard

Default blade

A light landing surface after selecting an organization. It explains the workspace areas and routes admins into focused blades.

  • 1Minimal summary with active holder, pending invite, credential type, and wallet challenge counts.
  • 2Quick action tiles for People, Credentials, Roles, Claims, Org chart, Integrations, Branding, and Ledger.
  • 3Dismissible administrator verification reminder with countdown context.
Open dashboard concept

People

Directory

The operational home for credential holders, invited users, admins, contractors, and revoked identities.

  • 1Paginated, searchable, filterable table with status, division, roles, and last wallet event.
  • 2Row actions for view, edit, re-invite, revoke, promote to co-admin, and send wallet challenge.
  • 3Clicking a record opens a details modal with claims, roles, QR invite, and transaction history.
Open people concept

Credentials

Issuance

Creates and tracks the invitation used by the Aegis mobile wallet to accept organization credentials.

  • 1Invite form with holder email, expiry override, assigned roles, division, and requested claims.
  • 2Generated handoff modal with QR code, deep link, copy action, and email or SMS send action.
  • 3Status panel showing waiting, accepted, expired, revoked, or failed invite state.
Open credentials concept

Roles

RBAC

Defines what a credential holder can do inside the organization and connected applications.

  • 1Role cards for Org Admin, Expense Approver, Employee, Contractor, and custom roles.
  • 2Add or edit role modal with privileges, eligible claims, divisions, and challenge policy.
  • 3Co-admin role assignment can require dual wallet challenge before it becomes active.
Open roles concept

Claims

Data model

Controls which claims can be issued, requested, shared by consent, or used in app authorization decisions.

  • 1Claim table with name, type, required state, consent mode, and connected use cases.
  • 2Add/edit claim modal for display name, data type, allowed values, source, and privacy behavior.
  • 3Support for delta claims when a new organization requests information not already shared.
Open claims concept

Org chart

Structure

A draw.io-style organization surface for divisions, departments, and default policy assignments.

  • 1Tree canvas for parent organization, divisions, departments, and sub-organizations.
  • 2Division form for default roles, requested claims, policy notes, and approver groups.
  • 3Credential issuance can inherit defaults from the selected organization node.
Open org chart concept

Branding

Wallet context

Lets each organization control the look of credentials and challenge context inside the mobile app.

  • 1Logo upload, palette presets, custom colors, and live wallet profile preview.
  • 2Default Vanguard palette plus selectable alternates and a custom palette option.
  • 3Branding is applied only while a holder is viewing that organization context.
Open branding concept

Integrations

Platforms

Houses setup wizards for Microsoft Verified ID, YubiKey/FIDO2, Keycloak, Okta, generic OIDC, and SAML.

  • 1One tile per provider with current state, live test, setup wizard, and callback guidance.
  • 2Verified ID fields for tenant, app registration, authority DID, manifest URL, and credential type.
  • 3YubiKey/passkey policy controls for sign-in, wallet approvals, signatures, and admin actions.
Open integrations concept

Wallet ledger

Evidence

Shows immutable wallet challenge activity for authentication, consent, approvals, promotions, signatures, and revocations.

  • 1Filterable event log with actor, resource, assurance level, timestamp, and evidence link.
  • 2Evidence modal can show payload, challenge ID, credential ID, passkey status, and request source.
  • 3Export and reporting actions support compliance and customer-facing audit packages.
Open ledger concept

Admin verification

Assurance

A step-by-step identity validation blade for original subscribers and co-admins.

  • 1Wizard step one uploads or captures government photo ID.
  • 2Wizard step two uses live webcam or front-facing camera face readiness checks.
  • 3Wizard step three reviews analysis and marks the admin profile validated or restarts.
Open verification concept

Settings

Policy

Controls organization-level defaults, retention, challenge requirements, and lifecycle operations.

  • 1Invitation default expiry, challenge policy, ledger retention, and privacy controls.
  • 2Disable organization without deleting evidence, or delete after explicit confirmation.
  • 3Policy cards can open modals for exact edits without overwhelming the page.
